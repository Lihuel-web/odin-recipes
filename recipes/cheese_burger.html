Cheese burger recipe 1

Description

Lasagna is a type of Italian dish made by layering flat pasta sheets with meat or vegetables,
cheese, and a sauce, typically a meat- or tomato-based sauce. The dish is then baked in the oven until the sauce is hot and bubbly and the pasta is cooked through.
The result is a hearty and flavorful meal that is often served with a side salad or garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • Italian sausage
  • onions
  • garlic
  • tomatoes
  • tomato sauce
  • mozzarella and Parmesan cheese
  • mushrooms
  • bell peppers
  • spinach
  • ricotta cheese
  • various herbs and spices

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. In a large saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, about 5 minutes.
  5. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and salt to the pan. Stir to combine and bring to a simmer.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, eggs, and chopped fresh basil.
  7. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the meat sauce.
  9. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  10. Repeat the layers of meat sauce, noodles, and ricotta cheese until you have used all of the ingredients.
  11. Top the lasagna with a final layer of meat sauce and sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  12. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 45 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and continue baking until the cheese is bubbly and golden, about 15 minutes.
  14. Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
